About Tanafs

Tanafs is an AI-powered healthcare project designed to assist medical professionals in intensive care units. It analyzes breathing patterns through medical image processing to detect abnormal conditions. The system helps reduce workload, improve monitoring accuracy, and support clinical decision-making. Tanafs contributes to advancing healthcare quality in alignment with Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030

The TANAFS System

Analyzes ventilator data to detect anomalies, helping ICU staff make faster and more accurate decisions.

Smart Monitoring

Tracks and visualizes patient breathing trends continuously to support proactive care and timely interventions.
