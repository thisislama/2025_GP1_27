About TANAFS

Tanafs is an AI-powered platform designed to assist healthcare professionals in intensive care units. It analyzes breathing patterns through medical image processing to detect abnormal conditions. The system helps reduce workload, improve monitoring accuracy, and support clinical decision-making. Tanafs contributes to advancing healthcare quality in alignment with Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030

TANAFS System

Designed to analyzes ventilator data for the specific purpose of pattern recognition and anomaly detection. It identifies characteristic breathing patterns and mark any significant abnormal respiratory events.

Empowering Healthcare Professionals

Designed to assist ICU teams by simplifying waveform interpretation, reducing cognitive load, and minimizing human error.

Advancing Digital Healthcare

Supports Saudi Vision 2030 by promoting innovation, digital transformation, and improved quality of patient care in the healthcare sector.
